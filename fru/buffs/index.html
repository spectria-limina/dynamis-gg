Appendix: Buffs

All Phases

 Damage Down Damage dealt is reduced. This is a 90% damage reduction.
Magic Vulnerability Up Magic damage taken is increased. Magic damage is lethal.
Mark of Mortality Branded with a mark of mortality. Damage dealt is reduced. Too many stacks will result in KO. This is a 90% damage down, which stacks with a regular Damage Down to produce a 99% damage down. Two(?) or more stacks is lethal.
Physical Vulnerability Up Physical damage taken is increased. Physical damage is lethal.

Phase 1

 Bleeding Sustaining damage over time. This is a very strong bleed from the raidwide.
Fated Burn Mark Emblazoned with a fateful burn mark that will cause you to explode with a vengeance when you are KO'd. If you die before the debuff falls off, it's a heavy raidwide with a Damge Down, but not an automatic wipe.
Floating Fetters Bound in midair and unable to move. You can still take actions.
Powder Mark Trail Emblazoned with a gunpowder mark that will cause you and the nearest ally to explode when this effect expires. Both players are hit by large AoE tankbusters. The explosions will go off immediately if the bearer dies.
Prey Marked as prey. You are tethered and will be hit by a mechanic from the tethered boss/clone.

Phase 2

 Chains of Everlasting Light Bound with chains of everlasting light. Will be subject to a Curse of Everlasting Light when this effect expires. You are tethered to two other players, but have no restrictions yet.
Curse of Everlasting Light Cursed by everlasting light. Close proximity to the player to whom you are bound will result in unaspected damage to those nearby. You are tethered to two other players.
Deep Freeze Your body is encased in ice, preventing action and dealing damage over time. Unable to move or take actions.
Lightsteeped Overflowing with astral energy. Upon accumulating 5 stacks, you will deal unaspected damage to those nearby. Wipes the party at 5 stacks.
Physical Vulnerability Up Physical damage taken is increased. Physical damage is lethal. Unlike in other phases, this can be cleansed with Esuna or Warden's Paean.
The Weight of Light Will unleash Powerful Light when this effect expires. A gold orb appears above your head. Powerful Light is light party stack AoE that inflicts one stack of Lightsteeped on anyone hit.
Thin Ice Having trouble maintaining a solid foothold upon ice-covered ground. Slip 'n' slide.

Phase 3

 Doom Certain death when counter reaches zero. Death really is certain.
Fire Resistance Down II Fire resistance is significantly reduced. Fire damage is lethal.
Return Aetherially entwined with the temporal manifold. You will be stunned and drawn back to your aetherial trace when this effect expires. When this expires, you will be returned to the snapshotted position and stunned for a few seconds.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Blizzard III Designated target of Dark Blizzard III, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Blizzard III is a donut AoE.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Eruption Designated target of Dark Eruption, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Eruption is a circle AoE.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Fire III Designated target of Dark Fire III, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Fire III is a large circle AoE.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Water III Designated target of Dark Water III, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Water III is a light party stack AoE. Will inflict a stack of Mark of Mortality if not soaked by 4+ players.
Spell-in-Waiting: Return Designated target of Return, which will activate when this effect expires. When this expires, your position will be snapshotted and a blue marker appears to indicate where it was snapshot. When the Return buff expires, you will be returned to the snapshotted position.
Spell-in-Waiting: Shadoweye Designated target of Shadoweye, which will activate when this effect expires. Shadoweye will inflict Doom, which is guaranteed death, on any player who isn't looking away.
Spell-in-Waiting: Unholy Darkness Designated target of Unholy Darkness, which will activate when this effect expires. Unholy Darkness is a large party stack. Will inflict Mark of Mortality if not soaked by 5+ players.
Stun Unable to execute actions. Can't move or take actions.

Phase 4

 Darkness's Design Subject to the Oracle of Darkness's design. Damage taken from certain targets is increased. Damage from the Usurper of Frost is lethal.
Light's Design Subject to the Oracle of Light's design. Damage taken from certain targets is increased. Damage from the Oracle of Darkness is lethal.
Lightsteeped Overflowing with astral energy. Upon accumulating 5 stacks, you will deal unaspected damage to those nearby. Wipes the party at 5 stacks.
Refulgent Chain Bound with chains of light. Your Refulgent Fate will be sealed when this effect expires. You are tethered to two other players, but have no restrictions yet.
Refulgent Fate Cursed by binding light. If the distance between you and the player to whom you are bound becomes too great or too small, you will deal unaspected damage to those nearby. You are tethered to two other players. You must stay at roughly the distance between the two towers.
Return Aetherially entwined with the temporal manifold. You will be stunned and drawn back to your aetherial trace when this effect expires. When this expires, you will be returned to the snapshotted position and stunned for a few seconds.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Aero III Designated target of Dark Aero III, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Aero III is a large circle AoE. Any other player hit by the AoE is knocked back a long way.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Blizzard III Designated target of Dark Blizzard III, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Blizzard III is a donut AoE.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Eruption Designated target of Dark Eruption, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Eruption is a circle AoE.
Spell-in-Waiting: Dark Water III Designated target of Dark Water III, which will activate when this effect expires. Dark Water III is a light party stack AoE. Will inflict a stack of Mark of Mortality if not soaked by 4+ players.
Spell-in-Waiting: Return Designated target of Return, which will activate when this effect expires. When this expires, your position will be snapshotted and a blue marker appears to indicate where it was snapshot. When the Return buff expires, you will be returned to the snapshotted position.
Spell-in-Waiting: Unholy Darkness Designated target of Unholy Darkness, which will activate when this effect expires. Unholy Darkness is a large party stack. Will inflict Mark of Mortality if not soaked by 5+ players.
Stun Unable to execute actions. Can't move or take actions.
Wyrmclaw Shredded by draconic talons. KO will occur when countdown reaches 0. Must be cleansed by touching a dragon head. This will cause an AoE and leave a blue puddle on the ground.
Wyrmfang Torn by draconic teeth. KO will occur when countdown reaches 0. Must be cleansed by touching a blue puddle left behind by someone cleansing Wyrmclaw.

Phase 5

 Dark Resistance Down II Dark resistance is significantly reduced. Dark damage is lethal.
Light Resistance Down II Light resistance is significantly reduced. Light damage is lethal.
